## 1. Automated Lead Gen and Qualification

The biggest leak in most Australian service businesses (from consulting to trades to real estate) isn't marketing; it's the follow-up.

When a lead comes in via a web form or Facebook Ad outside of business hours, waiting until the next morning to reply drops your conversion rate by over 60%.

We build systems using n8n and Make.com that capture that lead instantly. The system immediately cross-references their email to find their company data, sends a personalized email or SMS response within 60 seconds, and organizes the data perfectly into your CRM.

![Process Flow Outline](/images/blog/diagrams/flow-lead.svg)

If the lead is high-value, it books a Calendly meeting automatically.
